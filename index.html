Privacy Policy for NutriFit

Effective date: June 25, 2026
Last updated: June 25, 2026

NutriFit ("the app", "we", "us") is a nutrition and fitness tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used and stored, who it is shared with, and the choices and rights you have. We have written it to reflect what the app actually does — please read the section on AI photo and text analysis carefully, as it involves a third-party service.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

2. What data we collect

We only collect data you provide or generate by using the app. Specifically:

Account information

  • Email address (used to sign in and identify your account)
  • Name (the display name you enter at sign-up)
  • Account type — whether you use a personal ("private") account or a business/coach account

Profile and health information

When you complete onboarding or edit your profile, you may provide:

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Height
  • Current weight
  • Weight goal (lose / maintain / gain)
  • Activity level
  • Daily calorie and macronutrient (protein, carbs, fat) targets

Food log data

  • Food/meal descriptions you type or that are generated from a photo
  • Estimated calories and macronutrients (protein, carbs, fat) for each entry
  • The meal type (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack) and date

Food photos (for AI analysis)

When you use the photo-scan feature, the app sends the photo of your meal to an AI service to estimate its nutrition. See section 4 for important details on how these photos are processed. The app does not store your food photos on our servers — they are used only to produce an estimate and are not saved in our database.

Weight logs

  • Weight measurements you record over time, with their dates.

Workout data

  • Workout plans, workout days, exercises (sets, reps, planned/actual weights), and completed workout sessions you create or that a coach assigns to you.

Coach–client relationship (business accounts only)

If you use a business/coach account, the app stores the link between a coach and their clients, so that a coach can view the food, weight, and workout data of clients connected to them. If you are a client connected to a coach, that coach can see the data described above for your account.

We do not intentionally collect precise location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or payment information.

3. How we use your data

We use your data only to provide and operate the app's features, including:

  • Creating and securing your account and signing you in
  • Showing your food log, calorie/macro totals, weight trend, and workout plans
  • Estimating nutrition from your typed descriptions and meal photos
  • Allowing coaches to view and manage the data of clients connected to them
  • Maintaining and improving the reliability of the app

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

4. AI photo and text analysis — third-party processing (please read)

To estimate the nutrition of your meals, the app sends data to Google's Gemini API (operated by Google) for analysis:

  • When you scan a meal with a photo, the photo is sent to Google's Gemini API.
  • When you type a description of a meal (or a correction such as "also has tahini sauce"), that text is sent to Google's Gemini API.

This is necessary to generate the calorie and macronutrient estimate.

Important disclosure about Google's free tier. The app currently uses the free tier of Google's Gemini API. Under Google's terms for the free tier, the content you send (your meal photos and descriptions) may be used by Google to improve their products and machine-learning models, and may be reviewed by human reviewers as part of that process. This means a meal photo or description you submit could be seen by Google and used for model training, separately from how we store your data.

Because of this, please avoid including sensitive, personal, or identifying content in the photos you scan (for example, faces, documents, screens, or other people). Send only photos of the food itself.

This processing is governed by Google's own privacy terms, which are outside our control. You can review Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

If you are not comfortable with this, you can avoid the AI features and enter your food descriptions and nutrition numbers manually — the app fully supports manual entry and editing without any AI analysis.

5. How and where your data is stored

  • Your account and app data are stored using Supabase, a hosted backend platform built on the PostgreSQL database, which hosts the data on its cloud infrastructure. Our project is hosted in Supabase's EU (Frankfurt, eu-central-1) region.
  • Access to your data is protected by per-user Row Level Security (RLS) at the database level. This means the database itself enforces that you can only read and modify your own data — and, for coaches, the data of clients explicitly connected to them. Other users cannot access your data.
  • Authentication (sign-up and sign-in with email and password) is handled by Supabase Auth. Your password is managed and stored by Supabase in hashed form; we do not have access to your plaintext password.

6. Who your data is shared with

We share data only as needed to run the app:

  • Supabase — stores your account and app data and provides authentication.
  • Google (Gemini API) — receives meal photos and text descriptions you submit for AI nutrition analysis, as described in section 4.
  • Your coach — if you are a client connected to a coach on a business account, that coach can view your profile, food, weight, and workout data.

We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers, and we do not sell your data.

7. Data retention

  • We keep your account and app data for as long as your account exists, so the app can show your history (food log, weight trend, workouts).
  • Food photos are not retained by us — they are processed for an estimate and not stored in our database. (Note that Google may retain content sent to the free-tier Gemini API under its own terms; see section 4.)
  • When you delete individual entries (food, weight, workouts) they are removed from our database. When you delete your account, your data is deleted as described in section 8.

8. Your rights and choices

You have control over your data:

  • Edit your data — you can edit your profile, food entries, weight logs, and workout data directly in the app at any time.
  • Delete individual entries — you can delete food entries, weight logs, and other records in the app.
  • Delete your account and all associated data — to request deletion of your entire account and all data linked to it, email us at david9916d@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. When an account is deleted, the associated profile, food entries, weight logs, workout data, and coach–client links are removed from our database (our schema cascades deletion of these records when the account is removed).

Depending on where you live, you may also have rights under laws such as the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) — including the right to access, correct, export, or erase your data, and to object to or restrict processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email above.

9. Security

We rely on Supabase's infrastructure security and use database-level Row Level Security so each user can access only their own data. Data is transmitted to the backend and to the AI service over encrypted connections (HTTPS). The API key used to access the AI service is held only on the server side and is never shared with the app on your device.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a strong, unique password and keep it confidential.

10. Children's privacy

NutriFit is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Because the app collects health-related information, it is intended for adults (and, where permitted, teenagers using it with the involvement of a parent, guardian, or qualified coach). If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. Health information disclaimer

NutriFit provides estimates of calories and nutrition and general fitness tracking. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. AI estimates may be inaccurate. Always use your own judgment and consult a qualified professional for medical, dietary, or fitness decisions.
